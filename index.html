Recognition

Our Home Security System uses OpenCV, face_recognition,
and Python to scan live video feeds, recognizing authorized individuals from a stored dataset. Upon detecting a known face, it grants access and triggers alerts.
For unknown individuals, the system immediately initiates security measures by sending an email notification with an intruder's image, ensuring swift response to potential threats.
By seamlessly integrating facial recognition, our system offers efficient security and real-time monitoring, promising heightened safety and peace of mind for your home.
